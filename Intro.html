Dan Brown Movie Series

The Da Vinci Code trilogy consists of three films directed by Ron Howard and based on Dan Brown's bestselling novels featuring symbologist Robert Langdon (played by Tom Hanks): The Da Vinci Code (2006), Angels & Demons (2009), and Inferno (2016). These mystery thrillers follow Langdon as he deciphers ancient secrets in Europe.

• The Da Vinci Code (2006): Langdon (Tom Hanks) investigates a murder in the Louvre, unearthing secrets about the Holy Grail and the Catholic Church, aided by Sophie Neveu (Audrey Tautou).
• Angels & Demons (2009): Although the first book, the film acts as a sequel, following Langdon as he tackles a terrorist threat against the Vatican alongside physicist Vittoria Vetra (Ayelet Zurer).
• Inferno (2016): Langdon wakes up with amnesia in Italy, forcing him to follow clues related to Dante’s Inferno to prevent a global plague.
